# Product scope

The Trenches LeagueOS is organized around seven product pillars.

## Live League Hub

Standings, stat leaders, power rankings, playoff races, awards, and league history in one destination.

## Automated Game Threads

Matchup threads created from the weekly schedule with coach tags, analytics, activity tracking, scheduling, and game communication.

## Scouting and Draft Tools

Draft classes, coach big boards, draft primers, prospect intel, and live mock draft support.

## Trade Center

Trade blocks, structured submissions, committee voting, approvals, denials, and a searchable transaction history. Rules and approval authority remain commissioner-configurable.

## Coach Onboarding

Automatic team roles, welcome messages, quick-start setup, and open-team management.

## Weekly League Content

Awards, NFL-style matchup previews, Game of the Week visuals, weekly recaps, reporter posts, props, streams, draft grades, rankings, season statistics, and evolving storylines. Content is generated from structured league facts and published consistently to web and Discord after commissioner review.

## Systems

Recognition, coach perks, engagement tools, sportsbook-style features, and advanced league experiences. Spendable perks unlock private, opponent-specific analytics such as offensive and defensive game plans, tendency reports, scouting focus, and war-room intelligence. Sportsbook-style systems should use virtual league currency only unless legal and platform requirements are separately addressed.
